Technology

Arms and Tactics

  • Heavy Armor (HA): All infantry and cavalry except Peasants, Slaves, Direwolves, and Mammoths get +1 defense.
  • Improved Infantry Training (IT): Cost reduced by about 1/4 for all infantry.
  • Longbows (LB): Archers, HorseArchers, and Wildlings get +1 attack.
  • Valyrian Steel (VS): Knights and other upper-tier infantry and cavalry (usually base attack 3 or better) get +1 attack.
  • Volley Fire (VF): Archers and HorseArchers give +1 support on defense.

Engineering and Infrastructure

  • Advanced Fortification (AF): Walls give +1 defense to one defending infantry or artillery; Castles give +1 defense to up to three.
  • Grapeshot (GS): Catapults and Trebuchets roll twice for attack and defense.
  • Smithies (SM): Castles get +2 production in territories with value 3 or higher.
  • War Bonds (WB): Gain 1-12 extra PUs per turn.

Naval

  • Carrack Construction (CC): Allows production of Carracks; allows Dothraki to produce Galleys instead (at normal cost, even with Shipyards).
  • Improved Rigging (IR): All ships except Rafts get +1 movement.
  • Reinforced Hulls (RH): All ships except Rafts get +1 defense.
  • Shipyards (SY): Rafts' cost reduced by 1; all other ships' costs except Carracks reduced by 2; Dothraki can build Cogs (at normal cost).
  • Wildfire (WF): All ships except Rafts get +1 attack, and Galleys and Longships can bombard.
